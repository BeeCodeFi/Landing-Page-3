About Us

Trikha Weaving Factory has been manufacturing textile goods since 1978. Located on Chandigarh Road, Ludhiana, we specialise in art-silk, cotton cloth, knitted fabrics and shawls — offering bulk supply to retailers, wholesalers and institutions with quality checks at every stage.
